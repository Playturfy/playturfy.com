Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

PlayTurfy ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our football scoring and tournament management app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide the best app experience.

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect your name, email address, profile photo (optional), and account credentials through Firebase Authentication.

1.2 Usage & App Data

We collect:

  • Player names and details entered by users
  • Team names and logos
  • Match statistics (goals, cards, assists, substitutions)
  • Tournament data, fixtures, standings
  • Match summaries and history
  • News reading activity (non-identifiable)

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

Using Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics, we collect:

  • Device model and OS
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Performance data
  • Device identifiers (for authentication and analytics)

1.4 Uploaded Media

Users may upload team logos, player photos, and match summary cards. This content is fully user-generated.

2. Permissions We Use

PlayTurfy requests certain permissions to provide app functionality. These include:

  • Camera: For profile pictures and team logos
  • Storage/Photos: To upload images and save match summaries
  • Internet Access: To sync data with Firebase
  • Network State: To detect offline/online mode

We request permissions only when needed and explain them within the app.

3. User-Generated Content (UGC)

Users can create and upload:

  • Player names, team names, photos, logos
  • Match data and tournament data

Users are responsible for the content they upload. We reserve the right to remove any content that violates our policies, copyright, or laws.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide core match tracking and tournament features
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Generate statistics and match summaries
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure app security

We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

We use:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Firestore database (encrypted at rest and in transit)
  • Firebase Storage for media uploads

Regular security updates and audits are performed, and access is restricted through Firestore Security Rules.

6. Sharing of Information

We only share data with:

  • Firebase (Google) to operate app services
  • When required by law
  • When you choose to share match cards or summaries

We never share or sell personal data to advertisers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Edit or correct your information
  • Request account deletion
  • Withdraw optional permissions
  • Request a copy of your data

8. Account Deletion

To delete your account, email support@playturfy.com. Upon deletion:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account will be removed
  • Your Firestore data deleted within 30 days
  • Any media uploaded to Firebase Storage will be removed

9. Children's Privacy

PlayTurfy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a child account is discovered, we delete it immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

PlayTurfy relies on:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firestore Database
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Crashlytics
  • Google Play Services

These services have their own privacy policies and comply with GDPR/CCPA.

11. Google Play Data Safety Disclosure

As required by Google Play, the data collected includes:

  • Personal information (email, name)
  • App activity
  • Diagnostics

Data is encrypted, not shared except for Firebase service processing, and users can request deletion anytime. Optional data includes photos and logos uploaded by users. The purpose is app functionality, analytics, and crash reporting.

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any updates will reflect the new "Last Updated" date.
